Endsound by jkochis

Our goal is to create a browser based instrument / composition environment that anybody can join, create a room, and make music with anybody else on the internet. This also serves as a proof of concept for Mozilla's Audio data API. Our hope is that we can get enough people excited about it to advance the development of audio data APIs in the other rendering engines.
